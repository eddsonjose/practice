EDDSON JOSE PORTFOLIO ARTIST STATEMENT

Artist Statement

My work explores the infinite outcomes of new media while honing my skills with traditional ones. I seek to visualize my interpretation of global conundrums and goals that humankind would like to achieve. Influenced by issues such as pollution, construction over nature, and the recent boom in space exploration; my goal is to bring light to such issues while maintaining a positive outlook for the future. My recent works include digital art made through the process of code. In all aspects these works have imitated a look of outer space, coinciding with my inspiration of space exploration.
